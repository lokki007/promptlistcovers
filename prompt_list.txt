2. I'm an aspiring time traveler and concerned about surviving the zombie apocalypse in the future. How can I equip myself with the necessary skills and knowledge?

5. I'm a time traveler and face the challenge of surviving zombie outbreaks in the futuristic cyberpunk era. What futuristic gadgets and techniques should I employ for survival?
