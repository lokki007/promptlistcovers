## 6 AI Prompts: ADHD Management for Busy Parents

## Self-Awareness and Understanding
1. I have limited knowledge and understanding of ADHD and its impact on my life and parenting. How can I develop a deep understanding of ADHD and its effects on my life and parenting?
2. I struggle to recognize the symptoms and challenges associated with ADHD, which hinders my ability to effectively manage it. What steps can I take to identify and recognize the symptoms and challenges of ADHD?
3. Confronting the reality of living with ADHD feels overwhelming and I don't know where to start. How can I confront and accept the reality of living with ADHD in a positive and productive way?

## Strategies and Support
1. I need effective strategies to manage my ADHD symptoms and find support systems that work for me. What are some effective strategies and support systems that can help me manage my ADHD symptoms?
2. I struggle to find coping mechanisms, organizational tools, and lifestyle adjustments that work specifically for my ADHD. How can I discover coping mechanisms, organizational tools, and lifestyle adjustments that are tailored to my ADHD needs?
3. Putting in the effort to make positive changes and seeking help when needed feels challenging and overwhelming. What steps can I take to put in the effort to make positive changes and seek help when needed?
